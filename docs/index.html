Researching the global transition from intangible artificial intelligence to physical intelligence — where value shifts toward energy systems, advanced manufacturing, and industrial deployment.

From Intangible AI to Physical Intelligence

Sinclair Huang 是一位專注於人工智慧（AI）、實體基礎設施與資本市場交叉領域的研究員。他擁有 HEC Liège（列日大學管理學院）的企業管理高階博士學位 (EDBA)。

其研究重點在於新興市場跨國企業如何透過 AI 驅動的決策模型與全球佈局維持競爭優勢。

Sinclair Huang, PhD (EDBA), is an independent researcher specializing in AI-driven industrial competitiveness and capital markets. He holds an Executive Doctorate in Business Administration from HEC Liège and serves as Special Advisor to the Chairman at Continental Carbon Co., Ltd.
